Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

How much time will I need to spend daily?

Just 5 minutes a day. Our algorithm does all the heavy lifting, analyzing market trends and generating clear signals. You simply log in, review the recommended trades, and execute them with a click.

What exchanges are supported?

We currently support Binance, WazirX, CoinDCX, and Zebpay. We're constantly working to add more exchanges based on user demand.

How much capital do I need to start?

We recommend starting with at least ₹2,00,000 to achieve optimal results, but you can begin with as little as ₹50,000. The algorithm scales effectively with larger capital amounts.

Is this legal in India?

Yes, crypto trading is legal in India. Our algorithm simply automates your trading strategy and executes trades on your behalf. You maintain complete control over your accounts and funds.

How does your performance fee work?

We charge a 20% performance fee only on profits that exceed your portfolio's previous highest value (high watermark). This ensures you only pay for new profits and never pay fees twice on the same gains if your portfolio fluctuates.

What are the risks involved?

All trading involves risk, and cryptocurrency markets can be highly volatile. While our algorithm is designed to manage risk and capitalize on trends, past performance doesn't guarantee future results. You should only trade with capital you can afford to risk.
